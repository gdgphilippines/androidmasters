National Inter-School Android App Development Challenge

Android Masters is a collegiate inter-school Android app development challenge. It is open to all universities and colleges across the Philippines. The main objective of the program is to encourage students to develop their creative app ideas and publish it to Google Play Store.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does one participate in Android Masters?

Create a team composed of at most 3 students and a faculty mentor all coming from the same school/college/university. Students must be officially enrolled at the time of registration.

Is there a limit to the number of app entries per school?

There are no limits to the number of app entries per school. Teams can submit more than 1 entries across different categories.

Do the app we create need to be published?

Yes, it is a strict requirement that all entries should be published in the Google Play Store.

Can a mentor handle more than 1 team?

Yes, there are no limits to the number of teams handled by the mentors.

How many teams will qualify for the final round?

Top 5 entries per category will be invited for the culminating event in Manila on October 2015. Teams will pitch and demo their application to a panel of judges.

Can we submit finished apps but not yet published?

Yes, as long these apps were not submitted as part of any contest.

Where can I learn how to develop Android mobile applications?

You can visit the following websites to jumpstart learning in Android Development: Android Developer Official Site and Udacity courses Android for Beginners and Android Fundamentals.

How can we publish our apps?

To publish your apps on Google Play, you need to register for a Google Play developer account. Once registered, you can access the Google Play Developer Console. This is where you will upload and manage your Android apps.

Is there a fee for creating a Google Play developer account?

There is a one-time fee of $25 (less than 1, 250 pesos) for creating an account using a credit card or a debit card. You can you can use GCash MasterCard or SMART Money MasterCard if you do not have a credit nor debit card.

Can we update the apps we published and submitted before the deadline?

Yes, you may do so. The evaluation of the apps will start after the deadline of submissions.
