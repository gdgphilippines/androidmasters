About the Contest

Android Masters is an inititive of . It started as an inter-school Android app development challenge open to all senior high school to college students across the Philippines. The main objective of the initiative is to encourage students to develop creative app ideas and publish it to Google Play Store.

This year, we opened a new category for everyone with awesome and quality app that we’d like to call professionals (this can be anybody, freelancers, employees, or anyone with an awesome app).

Mechanics SENIOR HIGH AND COLLEGE LEVEL
  • School entries should be represented by a team of one (1) faculty adviser and up to three (3) students.
  • Teams must completely fill out and submit the Android Masters Application Form. Application deadline is September 15, 2019.
  • Student participants (college level) must be enrolled any time between academic year 2019-2020.
  • Each school can submit multiple entries provided that all of them meet the eligibility criteria/requirements set forth for the Competition.
  • Teams coming from the same school can have the same mentor/faculty adviser.
  • Qualified individuals are allowed to join as many teams they like for this Competition.
  • Teams can only submit a games application.
  • All submitted app entries should be published on the Google Play Store. A separate form will be emailed to all the participants to gather the Google Play Store link of the app. All apps must be published on or before September 30, 2019.
  • Top teams will be shortlisted and announced.
  • A National Finals Competition will be held to gather all Finalists to present their app to a panel of judges. Finalists will receive certificates and special prizes, while the category winners will receive Php 50,000. There will also be special awards for or being an all-female team.
  • Booths will be allocated for all the finalists and interested student teams to demonstrate their apps. Special award for the Best Booth.
  • Transportation and accommodation shall be shouldered by their respective schools.

PROFESSIONALS
  • You can be anyone. What matters is your awesome app and you becoming the Android Masters. Just form a team consists of not more than three (3) members.
  • Teams must completely fill out and submit the Android Masters Application Form. Application deadline is September 15, 2019.
  • Each team can submit multiple entries provided that all of them meet the eligibility criteria/ requirements set forth for the Competition.
  • Qualified individuals are allowed to join as many teams they like for this Competition.
  • Teams can either submit a utility/productivity or games application.
  • All submitted app entries should be published on the Google Play Store. A separate form will be emailed to all the participants to gather the Google Play Store link of the app. All apps must be published on or before September 30, 2019.
  • Top teams will be shortlisted and announced.
  • A National Finals Competition will be held to gather all Finalists to present their app to a panel of judges. Finalists will receive certificates and special prizes, while the category winners will receive Php 50,000. There will also be special awards for or being an all-female team.
  • Booths will be allocated for all the finalists and interested teams to demonstrate their apps. Special award for the Best Booth.
  • Transportation and accommodation shall be shouldered by their respective schools.
